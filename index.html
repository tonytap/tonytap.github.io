LP Morph (Code, Download)

"LP Morph" is a sound morphing plugin based on an efficient speech signal synthesis technique called linear predictive coding (LPC). The LPC model approximates a filter to represent the formant characteristics of input speech, then applies the filter on an excitation signal to synthesise a compressed version of speech. This plugin explores the creative potential of LPC by applying it on non-speech inputs and allowing real-time control of its parameters. The output sound imposes the characteristics of input on the excitation, often accompanied by a very glitchy feel.
